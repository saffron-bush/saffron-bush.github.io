* Everything is a Learnable Skill *

Do you believe in the theory that everything is a learnable skill?

This was the first thing I asked myself after the Pandemic hit and packed my bags and headed back to NZ to be with my family. I am now traversing the unknown by learning to code.

If you have stumbled across this blog, you also get to learn coding with me (Saffron).

Everything is a Learnable Skill is your virtual journey through the weird and wonderful world of coding with the belief that even you (yes, I am looking at you) can learn to code and that everything is a learnable skill
