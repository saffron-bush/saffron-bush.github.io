* Everything is a Learnable Skill *

Do you believe in the theory that everything is a learnable skill?

This was the first thing I asked myself after the Pandemic hit and I packed my bags to head back to NZ to be close to my family. I am now learning coding in a little over 4 and half months (with the help of Dev Academy) to prove the theory that everything is learnable, even coding.

If you have stumbled across this blog, you also get to learn coding with me (Saffron). Everything is a Learnable Skill is your virtual journey through the weird and wonderful world of coding. With the belief that even you (yes, I am looking at you) can learn to code
